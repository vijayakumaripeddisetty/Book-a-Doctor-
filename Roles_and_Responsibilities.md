# Roles and Responsibilities

## Overview

The **Book a Doctor** application involves three primary roles: **Patient (User)**, **Doctor**, and **Administrator (Admin)**. Each role has specific responsibilities to ensure efficient appointment management, secure communication, and smooth platform operation.

---

# 1. Patient (User) Responsibilities

The patient is responsible for using the application to manage healthcare appointments.

### Responsibilities

* Register using a valid email address and password.
* Create and maintain a personal profile.
* Search doctors by specialization, location, and availability.
* View doctor profiles and consultation details.
* Book appointments by selecting a preferred date and time.
* Upload medical records or supporting documents when required.
* Receive appointment confirmations and reminders.
* View appointment history.
* Cancel or reschedule appointments if necessary.
* Attend appointments on the scheduled date and time.
* View prescriptions and follow-up instructions after consultation.

---

# 2. Doctor Responsibilities

Doctors use the application to manage appointments and provide healthcare services.

### Responsibilities

* Register as a doctor on the platform.
* Maintain professional profile information.
* Update consultation timings and availability.
* Review appointment requests.
* Confirm or reject appointments.
* View patient medical records securely.
* Conduct consultations.
* Update appointment status.
* Record consultation notes.
* Upload prescriptions and follow-up recommendations.

---

# 3. Administrator (Admin) Responsibilities

The administrator manages and monitors the entire platform.

### Responsibilities

* Verify and approve doctor registrations.
* Manage patient and doctor accounts.
* Monitor appointments across the platform.
* Ensure only verified doctors are listed.
* Enforce platform policies and guidelines.
* Resolve user complaints and disputes.
* Maintain system security and data privacy.
* Monitor platform performance and reliability.
* Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations.

---

# Workflow Summary

1. Patient registers and logs into the application.
2. Patient searches for a suitable doctor.
3. Patient books an appointment and uploads required documents.
4. Doctor reviews and confirms the appointment.
5. Patient receives appointment confirmation.
6. Doctor conducts the consultation.
7. Doctor updates medical records and prescriptions.
8. Patient receives follow-up information.
9. Admin manages users, doctors, and platform operations.

---

# Conclusion

The Book a Doctor application clearly defines the responsibilities of patients, doctors, and administrators. Each role contributes to providing a secure, efficient, and reliable healthcare appointment management system.
